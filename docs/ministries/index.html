Our Ministries and Missions

Overseas Missions

We at Northwest are strongly committed to supporting those who have dedicated their lives to missionary work. Truly they are the hands and feet of Christ, honoring the Great Commission put forth by Jesus: "Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you." (Matthew 28:19-20)

We support a number of missions with monetary contributions and in prayer. In addition to our Faith Promise program of raising funds from congregation members, we also hold a number of fundraising events throughout the year to provide material support to these missions.
